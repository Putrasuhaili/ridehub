About UMPSA RideHub

UMPSA RideHub is a vehicle rental system developed to help students and staff of Universiti Malaysia Pahang Al-Sultan Abdullah (UMPSA) easily rent cars, scooters, motorcycles or bicycles on campus. Developed as part of the BCI2323 Web Programming course final project.
